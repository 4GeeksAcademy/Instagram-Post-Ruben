Banff National Park is one of the most popular parks in Canada. Here you can find crystal clear lakes, steep mountains and impressive mountain trails. It is the oldest national park in Canada and is part of the UNESCO World Heritage List. Its landscape is spectacular, with a variety of lakes of crystal clear waters such as Lake Moraine and Lake Louise, surrounded by imposing mountain peaks. During the winter, Banff National Park becomes a paradise for winter sports lovers, with world-renowned ski slopes and dreamy snowy landscapes.
